Technologies deployed
Electric shuttles E-bike sharing EV charging infrastructure Smart mobility tech Digital wayfinding
Project timeline
2026
Project secured and foundation phase
Wawasan Bumi establishes its PMO standards and ESG framework. The UM project is secured as the company's first flagship engagement. Design and planning commences.
2027
Pilot testing and initial deployment
Electric shuttle services, e-bike sharing systems, charging infrastructure and smart mobility technologies are piloted and deployed across the UM campus. This is the critical milestone for establishing Wawasan Bumi's market credibility.

Project performance reports, environmental outcomes and carbon reduction data are published. Independent ESG verification is completed. The UM project becomes the cornerstone portfolio case study for future client acquisition.
